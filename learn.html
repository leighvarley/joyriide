Introducing JoyRiide

A membership-based commuter bus that brings you from home to work while building a community around the people you commute with daily.
Pay an affordable monthly rate for:

Guaranteed seating

Wi-fi

Coffee and refreshments

Community

Environmental responsibility

Safety

Productivity

Life improvement

Once JoyRiide officially launches:

1. Sign up for a route.

2. Pay a $200/month flat fee for unlimited rides.

3. Download the JoyRiide mobile app to track the bus.

4. Bring joy to your commute!
